How it works

Unlock your daily coffee fix in 3 easy steps

01

Tell us your coffee preferences (and what you dislike)

Never again waste time thinking about what coffee to brew! Noure AI will create a 100% personalized weekly coffee plan just for you. It ensures you get all the flavors and caffeine you need, no matter your preference!

02

Approve your weekly coffee lineup

Once per week, approve the coffee plan generated for you by Noure AI. You can change blends, swap brewing methods, or even add your own recipes.

03

Receive coffee at your preferred time

Top baristas in town will brew your selected coffee every day, and we will deliver it to your door whenever works best for you. You can change delivery schedule and address daily!
